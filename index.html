Privacy Policy Details

This Privacy Policy explains how Push Up + ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your personal information when you use our mobile application, Push Up +, which is designed to help you develop your chest muscles through guided workouts and progress tracking.


1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not collect or store personal data unless you voluntarily provide it—for example, during registration, when you submit feedback, or contact support. Any information you share is used solely to improve your experience with the app.

Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information, such as device identifiers and app usage statistics, through third-party services. This data helps us understand how our app is used and to improve our services.


2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to enhance your experience with Push Up +, provide customer support, and communicate updates or improvements. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.


3. Third-Party Services

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Although we do not store significant personal data on our servers, we implement appropriate security measures to protect any information you provide. Please note that third-party services maintain their own security practices.


5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to review, update, or delete any personal information you have provided. If you have any concerns or requests regarding your data, please contact us using the details below.


6. Children's Privacy

Push Up + is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
